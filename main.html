Meet Your Trainer



My name is Sarah Hamar and I am born and raised from the Buffalo area. I l moved back to the 716 a few years ago and now reside in South Buffalo with my husband, Rob and our two kids, Declan and Scarlett. From a young age I have competed in travel/ elite athletics, specifically ice hockey and dance. During my high school years is when I was first introduced to dry land conditioning and working out. When I was a kid I did not understand the importance but looking back I realized that my love for fitness started to develop. When I was getting ready to go to college I hired my first personal trainer and I learned so much going into my freshman year. I went to SUNY Oswego where I was a Division III Women's Ice Hockey, Lacrosse and Rugby player. During my time there I spent the majority of my days perfecting my sport. But college ended as did competitive athletics.


I continued to implement fitness into my everyday life but it just didn't feel the same. A few years of this went on and I moved around, got married and had my son! Literally all in a few years. After having my baby I couldn't wait to get back in the gym! When I was able to lift again I went in full force and started to see really amazing changes in my physique and I decided to prep for a bikini competition. I competed in two shows 16 months after giving birth and won second in my first and first place in my second show and the rest is just history. I haven't stopped learning and focusing on my health and wellness since!


My main focus is to help individuals that need the extra push. I want to help you see what you are capable of doing and how strong you truly are physically and mentally. Along with personal training service I can offer lifestyle consultations to help move you in the right direction for success.
